What is Fundi Finance?

Fundi Finance is a yield generating hybrid for digital assets / security tokens / NFT’s, with services to tokenise physical assets into digital liquidity. Fundi boasts of being an advanced decentralised autonomous organisation governed by and built for its investing community. The DAO provides users true total control over their entire self-worth for the first time ever!
